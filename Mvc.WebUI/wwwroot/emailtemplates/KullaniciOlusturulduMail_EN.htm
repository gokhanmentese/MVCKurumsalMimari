MANUAL
First of all, just to make sure the system will work correctly, if your e-mail provider blocks the pictures, please unblock them and add this e-mail address to safe senders list. If you have any hesitation about this, please contact your sales director as soon as possible!
Please note that the deadline for completing all the procedures is 28 / 12 / 2017 , and later than this date, it will not be possible to enter the system and your company information will not take place on the Exhibition Catalogue, etc. Thank you in advance for your sensitivity. 
  1. Please open the login link below with the latest version of Google Chrome or Internet Explorer. You can download Google Chrome here.
  2. Following your login, before filling the forms, please read "Exhibitor Manual" and "Technical Services Manual" under the Forms and Links tab at the top of the page. 
  3. Please click on My profile link and fill in necessary information on "My Profile" page by considering the explanation at the top of the page.   
  4. After completing the previous step, you can create "Your Badges" you can use during the exhibition time on the Procedures tab.
  5. Exhibitor list on the system will keep getting detailed as exhibitors proceed on their procedures and updated constantly. Exhibitor list will be available to access until the exhibition begins. 
  6. In case of any system error, you can contact your sales representative or send an e-mail to destek@ite-ekinfuar.com.
